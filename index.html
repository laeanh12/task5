History

A meat shop, also known as a butcher shop or butcher's store, is a retail establishment that specializes in the sale of various meat products. These establishments typically source, prepare, and sell a variety of meats, including beef, pork, lamb, poultry, and sometimes other specialty meats. It's important to note that the specific features and offerings of a meat shop can vary widely based on its size, location, and the preferences of its customer base.
